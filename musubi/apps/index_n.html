Users will invite others on the feed to the game. Each user will submit a Truth and a Dare at the start of the game. One game will be comprised of two rounds. The first round will allow each player to choose between receiving a Truth or a Dare, chosen from those compiled at the start of the game. In the second round, the players will automatically receive a Dare if they chose a Truth for the first round, and vice versa. For Dares, players must submit a video or photo as evidence of completion, or they may choose to Pass. For Truths, players must respond to the question posed. Whether it be a truth or a dare, the players' actions will be posted onto the feed.
